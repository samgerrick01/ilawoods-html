About Ilawoods

The Ilawoods Resort and Sanctuary is a secluded eco-resort located in Dayapan, Lemery, Batangas. The resort boasts beautiful natural surroundings, including lush greenery and a pristine pool, making it the perfect destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and reconnect with nature. The resort offers a variety of amenities, including comfortable accommodations, and recreational activities such as and swimming and arcade games like Piso Vendo.


Ilawoods Resort and Sanctuary is also dedicated to preserving the natural environment and promoting sustainable tourism practices. Ilawoods Resort and Sanctuary offers a variety of amenities to its guest, including comfortable accommodations that feature modern amenities and breathtaking views of the natural surrounding. Guest can take part in recreational activities and explore the surrounding natural emvironment. The resort also offers special packages for events such as weddings, birthdays and corporate retreats. Additionally, Ilawoods Resort and Sanctuary is committed to promoting sustainable tourism practices, making it a great choice for travelers who are looking for an eco-friendly and sustainable vacation. Overall, the resort offers a range of activities and facilities to cater yo the needs of different types of guest and it's an ideal place for travelers who want to experience an eco-friendly and serene environment.
